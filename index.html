About me

I am an applied scientist at Amazon Search. I am interested in developing scalable and efficient algorithms for large-scale machine learning problems, including extreme multi-label learning and deep neural network models.

I received my Ph.D. from the Oden Institute at UT Austin under supervision of Prof. Inderjit S. Dhillon. Before that, I got my B.S. from Tsinghua University.
